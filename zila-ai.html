ZILA-AI

ZILA-AI is the artificial intelligence branch of the ZILA ecosystem. Its purpose is to integrate AI into decentralized finance operations, automate smart contract monitoring, and enable predictive analytics for traders and stakers.

ZILA and ZILA-AI together aim to create a robust, scalable ecosystem where community governance, transparency, and AI-powered insights drive decisions for the next-generation DeFi.

Users can interact with ZILA-AI for automated staking suggestions, portfolio tracking, and task verification to earn points in the ecosystem.
